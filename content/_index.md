I am associate dean of academic affairs and associate professor of law (with tenure) at [Chicago-Kent College of Law, Illinois Institute of Technology](https://www.kentlaw.iit.edu/). My research focuses on securities law, administrative law, and legal analytics. Before entering the academy, I was senior counsel at the SEC's Office of the General Counsel and practiced appellate litigation at Mayer Brown LLP.

My work has been published in the *Yale Law Journal*, *Duke Law Journal*, *Stanford Journal of Law, Business, and Finance*, and the *Michigan State Law Review*, among others. I have been quoted in the *Wall Street Journal*, *Financial Times*, *Barron's*, *Law360*, *CNN Business*, and *Mother Jones*.
